## Superseded by ADRs (Remove from v1)

These REQs have been replaced by documented decisions. They should be deleted from `REQUIREMENTS.md` (or moved to v2):

| REQ-ID | Original Spec | Superseded by | Reason |
|---|---|---|---|
| REGS-03 | imperative `mesh.register(name, channel, handler, ...)` | **ADR-0030** | `AgentSpec` + `@mesh.agent(spec)` is the registration path. Decorator-only is the documented decision. |
| REGS-07 | heartbeat on `mesh.health.{channel}.{name}` | **ADR-0033** + **ADR-0016** | ADR-0033 explicitly defers health to a future ADR ("framework-level ping subject... out of scope"). ADR-0016 (disconnect advisories) is the alternative. Subject path is also invalid post-ADR-0049 (no channel). |
| LIFE-02 | `mesh.start()` / `mesh.stop()` | **ADR-0022** + ADR-0034 code samples | The canonical lifecycle is `async with mesh:` (context manager). Explicit start/stop never made it into any ADR; all documented samples use `async with`. **User confirmation needed before removing.** |
